# Synthetic And Screenshot-Inspired Generation

The demo dataset is generated by `scripts/build_dataset.py`.

The builder creates fixed groups:

- harsh allowed movie criticism
- actor and director work criticism
- direct user/reviewer attacks
- fandom and comment-section group attacks
- sensitive political/religious discussion
- threat-like comments
- neutral discussion
- ambiguous Hinglish replies
- small sanitized open-source sample

The random seed is fixed at `42`.

Screenshot-inspired examples are not copied from screenshots. They are rewritten scenario examples based on observed patterns:

- direct replies
- rating disagreements
- `@user` mentions
- Hinglish tone
- review-thread arguments
- movie-craft criticism

No raw screenshot text, names, handles, avatars, or timestamps are stored.
